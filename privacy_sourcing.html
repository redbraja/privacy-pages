Privacy Policy

This policy describes how this internal product research tool (the "Application"), which uses the eBay Browse API, handles information.

Information We Collect

The Application retrieves only publicly available item listing data via the eBay Browse API (item title, price, category, and images) for internal product sourcing research. The Application does not collect or store any personal information of eBay members, including buyer information, seller account/profile information, or order and transaction records.

Purpose of Use

Retrieved public listing data is used solely for internal product sourcing and pricing research.

Retention and Deletion

Because the Application does not store personal information of eBay members, no personal data retention or deletion procedures apply.

Third-Party Sharing

Retrieved data is not shared with or redistributed to third parties.
