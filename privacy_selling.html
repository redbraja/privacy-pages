Privacy Policy

This policy describes how this application (the "Application") handles information in connection with its use of eBay APIs to manage the seller's own eBay store listings.

Information We Collect

The Application uses eBay APIs (Inventory / Offer / Listing) solely to create, update, and delete product listings in the seller's own eBay store (item title, price, description, images, item specifics, and quantity). The Application does not collect, store, or process any personal information of eBay members, including buyer names, shipping addresses, order details, or payment information.

Purpose of Use

Listing data is used exclusively to manage the seller's own product catalog on eBay.

Retention and Deletion

Because the Application does not collect personal information from eBay members, no personal data retention or deletion procedures apply.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with or disclosed to third parties.
